Who am I?

I am educated as a civil engineer in Software Engineering at the University of Southern Denmark. I chose Software Engineering as the education I want to study in, as there are plenty of opportunities to gain knowledge throughout my life. Furthermore, as you probably know, software is something that is constantly evolving and I find it quite exciting that there is always something new to get into.
